VIGOUR - Player "SP"
	-MAX 100
	-Basic moves consumes 20
	-Skill moves consumes 40
	-SPECIAL moves do not cost vigour
	-Half the cost is refunded if the attack hits a weakness

	-Ends player turn when vigour is 0

GIRT - Enemy "toughness"
	-Multiple stacks
	-Reduces GRIT when weakness hit
	-Losing a stack deals additional damage
	-Losing all stacks gives the party max vigour
