VIGOUR - Player "SP"
	-MAX 100. Shared across team
	-Basic moves consumes 20
	-Skill moves consumes 40
	-SPECIAL moves do not cost vigour

	-Ends player turn when vigour is 0
	-Resets back to 100 when enemy turn finished

GRIT - Enemy "toughness"
	-Multiple stacks
	-Reduces GRIT when player hits their weakness
	-Losing a stack deals additional damage and refunds half move cost
	-Losing all stacks gives the party max vigour and reduces cost of all moves by half for 	 that turn
